About Us

Just five dudes against the world. We all attended UAA (University of Alaska-Anchorage) together for half a semester until we ran out of money from all our pizza and cuddling. Cuddling is expensive. One day, we had the brilliant idea to start a website where people who love cuddling (and nothing more) could come to bond and feel safe in their innocence. And so, Snuggler was born and we've been running it ever since. Thank you all for your support!
